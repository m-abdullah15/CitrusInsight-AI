About Us

Citrus farming is a vital sector in agriculture, but farmers often face significant challenges in maximizing yield and maintaining high-quality produce due to a lack of timely, reliable guidance. Currently, there is no comprehensive platform that provides farmers with month-wise recommendations for fertilizer, pesticide use, irrigation schedules, or disease management based on real-time data. Furthermore, farmers often struggle to manage the cost-effectiveness of land preparation, planting density, and long-term maintenance of citrus orchards. Despite some progress in agricultural technology, such as crop disease detection and yield prediction, there remains a gap in integrating all necessary tools into one accessible system for farmers.
Our project aims to address these gaps by creating an integrated citrus recommendation system that not only provides guidance on irrigation, fertilizers, and pesticides, but also incorporates disease prediction through machine learning algorithms using citrus leaf datasets.
